Healthcare: Secure Patient Data Sharing

Healthcare organizations are implementing blockchain to create secure, interoperable health records while maintaining patient privacy and regulatory compliance.

Patient-Controlled Access

Patients maintain complete control over who can access their health data, with granular permissions and audit trails for every access event.

Interoperability Across Systems

Seamless data sharing between hospitals, clinics, and specialists while maintaining data integrity and patient consent requirements.

Drug Traceability & Authentication

End-to-end pharmaceutical supply chain tracking to prevent counterfeit drugs and ensure medication authenticity.

Real Estate: Fractional Ownership & Smart Contracts

The real estate industry is being transformed through tokenization, enabling fractional ownership and automated property management through smart contracts.

Revolutionary Use Cases:

Property Tokenization

Convert real estate assets into digital tokens, enabling fractional ownership and improved liquidity for property investments.

Automated Rent Collection

Smart contracts automatically collect rent, distribute payments to owners, and manage property maintenance funds.

Transparent Property History

Immutable records of ownership transfers, renovations, and property valuations accessible to all stakeholders.

Escrow Automation

Eliminate traditional escrow services with smart contracts that automatically release funds upon contract condition fulfillment.
